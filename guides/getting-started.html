Getting Started with ZGRAD

By ZGRAD Team January 1, 2024

Welcome to ZGRAD!

This guide will help you get started with playing on ZGRAD servers. We'll cover the basics of connecting, understanding game modes, and getting involved in the community.

1. Connecting to ZGRAD

To join our servers:

  • Open Garry's Mod
  • Click on 'Find Multiplayer Game'
  • Search for 'ZGRAD' in the server browser
  • Join any of our servers!

2. Understanding Game Modes

ZGRAD offers several unique game modes:

  • HOMIGRAD: Our flagship game mode featuring intense team-based combat
  • Sandbox: Creative mode where you can build and experiment
  • Custom Events: Special events hosted regularly by our staff team

3. Getting Involved

Join our community:

  • Connect to our Discord server
  • Follow us on social media
  • Participate in community events
  • Share your feedback and suggestions
