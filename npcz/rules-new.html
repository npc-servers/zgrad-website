SERVER GUIDELINES

  1. Do not harass/intentionally annoy other players.
  2. Mic Spamming/earraping is not allowed.
  3. We are an english speaking server, so keep all-chat in english. You can use other languages in PMs/Near/Squads.
  4. Modifying your game to provide an unfair advantage (cheating/exploiting) is against the rules.
  5. Intentionally lagging/crashing the server is not allowed.
  6. Threatening or attempting to dox/DDoS other players will result in a permanent ban.
  7. Advertising/recruiting in the server (Discord invites, clan recruiting, or server promotion) is not allowed.
  8. NSFW Content is not allowed.
  9. Do not engage in/use offensive behavior or language.
  10. Do not break rules because someone else is breaking them; you will also be punished.
  1. Do not prop push or prop kill under any circumstances.
  2. Using props/vehicles to enter peoples bases is not allowed.
  3. Do not prop spam, doing this may result in a ban without warning!
  4. Blocking off large parts of the map, including roads and spawnpoints, is not allowed.
  5. Do not build in the skybox.
  6. Do not spawn props/entities in or on a player's build/base without permission. This includes spawnpoints.
  7. Do not use props or balloons to gain access to a player's build/base. Propflying for map traversal is allowed.
  1. You may not use PVE mode to gain, or provide another player(s) any form of advantage in PVP.
  2. In PVP, equipping a smaller than average playermodel while in pvp mode is not allowed, same goes for playermodels that look normal but have smaller than usual hitboxes. If you are in PVE mode you are exempt from this rule.
  3. Attacking players from outside the map or inside a space that isn't accessible is not allowed.
  4. While in PVE mode you are not allowed to kill other players in any way.
  5. Do not use PVE mode to body block entries or areas.
  6. While in PVE mode, you must leave a PVP player's base if they ask you to.
  1. In PVP, doors must have an easily accessible and visible keypad or button within close proximity for entering.
  2. In PVP, you are not allowed to use keypads to disorient a raider (eg. multiple keypads for 1 door)
  3. Using props to block off areas without keypads is not allowed. If you are in PVE mode you are exempt from this rule.
  4. If you have a base in PVE you cannot let PVP players shoot out of it.
  5. Bases must be completely enterable without crouching/obstruction.
  6. Bases can have a maximum of 4 doors per entrance.
  1. If you wouldn't fight it, don't use it! Anything built for malicious intent is not allowed.
  2. Contraptions designed to automatically kill zombies in a large radius are not allowed. You are exempt from this rule if you are base building.
  3. If the intentions of the contraption is to fight with it you MUST have contraption health core present on the contraption. (see the construction tools section)
  4. Contraptions should be equal in power to their Simfphys and LFS counterparts.
  5. Drone contraptions are not allowed. You can use our counterparts in the entities tab or the !shop.
  6. Player targeting is not allowed. Using radars, Tracking Turrets, commands, or anything related is not allowed. This also includes player blinders and teleportation jails.
  7. Builds and contraptions must be optimized: paste without constraints, Prop2Mesh it, or rebuild it to be simpler. If your build degrades server or client performance, it will be deleted.
